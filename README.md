

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Online Retail Sales Analysis** performed using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective was to clean raw retail transaction data, analyze sales performance, build interactive reports, and create a professional dashboard for business decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical data analytics skills including data cleaning, transformation, aggregation, visualization, dashboard development, and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary goals of this analysis were to:

* Understand overall sales performance.
* Identify revenue trends across time periods.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate country-wise sales distribution.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Create an interactive dashboard for quick business monitoring.
* Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains online retail transactions including:

* Invoice information
* Product details
* Customer information
* Sales quantities
* Unit prices
* Country information
* Transaction dates

The analysis covers sales activity across **38 countries** and multiple product categories.

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Strong Overall Business Performance

* The business generated approximately **$10.6M** in total revenue.
* Nearly **20,000 invoices** were processed, indicating a large transaction volume.

### 2. Clear Seasonal Demand Pattern

* Sales activity peaks during **September to November**.
* These months represent the strongest revenue-generating period of the year.

### 3. High Geographic Concentration

* Revenue is heavily concentrated in the domestic market.
* The business currently relies on a limited geographic revenue base, creating concentration risk.

### 4. Product Revenue Concentration

The following products are among the highest contributors to sales:

* DOCTOM POSTAFE
* REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
* PAPER CRAFT
* LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these products contribute a significant share of total sales, indicating that a small group of products drives a large portion of revenue.

### 5. International Growth Opportunity

* Sales are distributed across **38 countries**.
* International markets provide opportunities for future expansion and revenue diversification.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Expand International Presence

Reduce dependence on the domestic market by increasing focus on high-potential international regions.

### Inventory Planning for Peak Season

Prepare inventory and logistics capacity ahead of the September–November sales surge.

### Product Portfolio Diversification

Reduce reliance on a small group of top-performing products by expanding and promoting additional product lines.

### Customer Retention Programs

Develop loyalty and retention initiatives to increase repeat purchases and customer lifetime value.

### Monitor Geographic Risk

Regularly track revenue concentration to ensure sustainable growth across multiple markets.

## ⚠️ Data Considerations

* December 2011 data may be incomplete within the dataset.
* Any decline observed during December should **not be interpreted as a confirmed business slowdown without additional validation**.
* Insights should be considered within the context of the available data period.
